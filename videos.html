WHO?

We are middle schools' students, from Alabama Auburn City schools. Our team 36849K was composed in October 2022 by SCORE (Southeastern Center of Robotics Education).

MORE ABOUT US

After intensive training and careful preparation, we won the 1st at 2022 Fall ARC (Auburn Robotics Club ) League Finals - Vex IQ Slapshot, 2nd at the tournament in Selma, AL, and 1st at the tournament in Auburn, AL. The 5th ranking in the Alabama championship qualified us to VEX Robotics World Championship 2023.
